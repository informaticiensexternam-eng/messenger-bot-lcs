Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Messenger_Bot_LCS application (hereinafter "the Application") collects, uses, and protects information from users who interact with the operator's Facebook Page through Messenger.

1. About Us

The Application is operated by an independent operator using Meta's Messenger platform to automatically respond to messages received on their professional Facebook Page. For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: informaticiens.externam@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

When you send a message to our Facebook Page, the Application may receive and process:

  • Your Messenger user identifier (PSID) provided by Meta;
  • Your first name, last name, and public profile picture;
  • The content of the messages you send us (text, attachments, emojis);
  • Timestamps of the exchanges;
  • Detected language and certain technical metadata required for the conversation.

We do not collect your email address, phone number, friends list, or any sensitive data (health, political opinions, religion, etc.).

3. Purposes of Processing

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your messages in an automated and personalized manner;
  • Maintain conversation continuity (memory of the conversation thread);
  • Improve the quality of customer service;
  • Comply with our legal obligations where applicable.

4. Use of an Artificial Intelligence Provider

To generate its responses, the Application transmits the content of your messages to Anthropic PBC (Claude API), an AI provider located in the United States. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/legal/privacy. Data sent to Anthropic is not used to train its models, in accordance with the API terms currently in effect.

5. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on your consent, expressed by voluntarily sending a message to our Facebook Page, as well as on our legitimate interest in providing an effective customer service.

6. Data Retention

Messages and associated metadata are retained for a maximum period of 90 days, unless the law requires longer retention or you request earlier deletion. Beyond this period, data is permanently deleted.

7. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your data. The only third parties that may receive your information are:

  • Meta Platforms Inc. as the operator of Messenger;
  • Anthropic PBC for generating responses (see Section 4);
  • Competent authorities in case of legal obligation.

8. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable regulations (notably the GDPR for European residents), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data;
  • Right to rectification;
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten");
  • Right to restriction of processing;
  • Right to data portability;
  • Right to object;
  • Right to withdraw your consent at any time.

9. How to Delete Your Data

Deletion request:

To request the deletion of all your data, send a message containing the keyword DELETE (or SUPPRIMER) to our Facebook Page, or email us at informaticiens.externam@gmail.com specifying your Facebook name. We will process the deletion within a maximum of 30 days and send you a confirmation.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data: encryption of communications (HTTPS/TLS), restricted server access, and access logging. As no system is infallible, however, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Minors

The Application is not intended for persons under 13 years of age. If you believe a minor has shared data with us, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. The last updated date appears at the top of the document. In the event of a material change, we will notify you via a Messenger message.

13. Contact

For any questions, complaints, or to exercise your rights, contact us at:
informaticiens.externam@gmail.com
